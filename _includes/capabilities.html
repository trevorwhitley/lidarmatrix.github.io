Roadside LiDAR data collection

Portable, non-intrusive sensing platforms capture continuous multimodal movement across intersections, corridors, crossings, school and work zones, and other transportation facilities in urban and rural environments — with an effective sensing radius of up to 300 feet over deployment periods ranging from days to weeks.

Multimodal counts

Automated processing generates multimodal counts and behavioral metrics for vehicles, pedestrians, bicycles, and scooters — including turning movements, crossing activity at and away from crosswalks, path utilization, and compliance.

Operational metrics

Automated analytics extract operational performance metrics — peak-hour volumes, peak-hour factors, queue lengths, travel times, delays, and curb-management / parking compliance — and other measures of intersection, corridor, and network operations.

Surrogate safety metrics

Advanced analytics quantify surrogate safety performance through conflict and near-miss detection, yielding behavior at uncontrolled crossings, speed analysis, exposure-risk assessment, and compliance with signals, signs, and roadway movements.

Before-and-after studies

Paired one-week before / one-week after campaigns to quantify the safety and operational impact of a treatment.

Grant & agency support

Data collection, analytics, and technical support for SS4A, SMART, Vision Zero, Safe Routes to School, Neighborhood Plans, and other federal, state, and local transportation programs — as a prime or sub-consultant.
