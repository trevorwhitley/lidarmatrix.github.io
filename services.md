<section class="section">
  <div class="wrap">
    <p class="lead">Every engagement is built around a question you need to answer: Is this crossing safe? Should this left turn run protected or permissive? Where should the next investment go? Our services translate raw LiDAR point clouds into the measures that answer it.</p>

    <div class="feature">
      <h3><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path d="M3 12h4l2-7 4 14 2-7h6"/></svg>Roadside LiDAR data collection</h3>
      <p>Portable, non-intrusive roadside sensors deployed at signalized and unsignalized intersections, midblock crosswalks, corridors, and school zones. Continuous, around-the-clock detection of every road user across a wide radius, in urban and rural contexts alike — with no lane closures or in-pavement hardware.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="feature">
      <h3><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path d="M12 2a10 10 0 1 0 10 10"/><path d="M12 12l6-3"/><circle cx="12" cy="12" r="1.6"/></svg>Trajectory &amp; traffic-safety analytics</h3>
      <p>AI methods convert raw point clouds into fully anonymous, all-road-user trajectories tracked at 10 Hz — object classification, position, speed, acceleration, and dimensions. From there we derive conflict and near-crash events, driver yield rates, gap acceptance, red-light running, vehicle speeds and headways, queue lengths, and other surrogate-safety measures.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="feature">
      <h3><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path d="M4 19V5"/><path d="M4 19h16"/><path d="M8 16v-5"/><path d="M13 16V8"/><path d="M18 16v-3"/></svg>Turning-movement &amp; multimodal counts</h3>
      <p>Continuous vehicle turning-movement and pedestrian- and bicyclist-crossing counts, delivered as queryable data feeds or live web dashboards. Replace episodic manual counts with data your engineers can view or download on demand to support day-to-day operations, signal-timing decisions, and safety screening.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="feature">
      <h3><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path d="M12 3l8 4v5c0 5-3.5 8-8 9-4.5-1-8-4-8-9V7z"/><path d="M9 12l2 2 4-4"/></svg>Real-time detection &amp; actuation</h3>
      <p>Sensor-based, real-time detection of pedestrians and cyclists at crossings — used to automatically trigger rectangular rapid flashing beacons (RRFBs) or support pedestrian-actuated signal control without a push button. Detection logic is tuned to true crossings to avoid false triggers, with adaptive turn-off once a crossing clears.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="feature">
      <h3><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path d="M3 3v18h18"/><path d="M7 15l4-5 3 3 5-7"/></svg>Before-and-after evaluation studies</h3>
      <p>Rigorous study designs pairing one-week before and one-week after data campaigns with seasonal control sites, so measured changes reflect the treatment and not the season. Ideal for evaluating signal-phasing changes, warning systems, queue detection, and other safety countermeasures.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="feature">
      <h3><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path d="M12 3l9 5-9 5-9-5z"/><path d="M3 12l9 5 9-5"/><path d="M3 16l9 5 9-5"/></svg>Grant &amp; agency support</h3>
      <p>Independent, third-party data collection and evaluation for federally funded programs — including Safe Streets and Roads for All (SS4A) and USDOT SMART grants — as prime recipient or sub-consultant. Deliverables include trajectory and raw datasets, data management plans, user manuals, and data dictionaries.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
